Hi, I'm a professional software developer specializing in JavaScript/HTML5 Applications.

I’ve provided software development services to several companies, startups and agencies including SunTrust, Staples, Tide and AMC Networks; thewonderfactory.com and kernandlead.com, interactive agencies in New York City; jsgeeks.com, a London-based company providing specialized software services in web and mobile areas; gridium.com, energy data analytics startup based in Menlo Park; bureauoftrade.com, an e-commerce startup based in Palo Alto; plummediagroup.com, a performance based online media agency. Specialties: JavaScript, HTML5, Node.js, REST APIs, Mongo, Architecture, Continuous Delivery, TDD, Rapid Prototyping and Web Analytics.

Spoken Languages: English, Portuguese, Italian and Spanish.
